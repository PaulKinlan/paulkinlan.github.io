--- layout: post title: Untitled published: true --- Following my post about a lack of bluetooth communications libraries for desktop style pc's. Mark Arteaga has pointed me towards 32Feet.net, which has a managed library. I have not had a chance to look at it just yet, but when I do I will let you know what it is like.

From the sample it looks pretty simple to use. Discovering devices looks like it can return an array of bluetooth accessbile systems via: BluetoothDeviceInfo[] bdi = bc.DiscoverDevices(12); (not too sure what 12 means, but it might be the maximum number of devices). All the communication appears to be stream based.
